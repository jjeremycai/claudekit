Help me shop on Taobao (taobao.com) using browser automation.

## Your Sizing Reference

When recommending sizes or filtering products, use these measurements:
- Height: 183 cm (6'0")
- Weight: 173 lbs (~78 kg)
- Jacket size: 38R (EU 48, CN 175/92A)
- Waist: 31 inches (~79 cm)
- Inseam: 32 inches (~81 cm)
- Shoe size: US 9.5-10 (EU 43, CN 270-275)
- Shirt size: Medium or Large (depending on cut)

**Chinese size conversions:**
- Tops: L or XL (175/92A or 180/96A)
- Pants: 31-32 (79-81 cm waist, 81 cm inseam)
- Shoes: 43 or 270mm

## Workflow

### 1. Navigate to Taobao
- Go to taobao.com
- Handle any region/language popups

### 2. Search for Products
- Translate the search query to Chinese if needed
- Enter the search term
- Apply relevant filters (size, price, ratings)

### 3. Browse Results
- Summarize top results with:
  - Product name (translated)
  - Price (in CNY and approx USD at current rate)
  - Store rating/reviews
  - Available sizes
- Highlight products that match my sizing

### 4. Product Details
When I select a product:
- Show size chart and recommend my size based on measurements
- Translate key product details
- Show shipping options/costs
- Note any warnings (sizing runs small/large, seller reputation)

### 5. Purchase Assistance
- Help add correct size to cart
- Do NOT complete purchases - stop before payment
- Show cart summary before any checkout action

## Translation Guidelines

- Translate Chinese product titles and descriptions to English
- Keep brand names in original form
- For sizing, always show both Chinese and Western equivalents
- Translate seller communications/size guides
